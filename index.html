Part A: Definition and Basic Rules

Session 1: Introduction to Derivatives

Video Content:

Definition of the Derivative - Complete worked example of $f(x) = \frac{1}{1+x^2}$ using the limit definition

Key Concepts:

  • Derivative as limit of difference quotient: $f'(x) = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x + \Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x}$
  • Geometric interpretation as slope of tangent line
  • Even functions have odd derivatives

Part B: Implicit Differentiation and Inverse Functions

Session 13: Implicit Differentiation

Video Content:

Implicit Differentiation - Complete example: Find slope of tangent to $y^3 + x^3 = 3xy$ at point $(4/3, 2/3)$

Key Concepts:

  • Chain rule applied to implicitly defined functions
  • Product rule for terms like $3xy$
  • Solving linear equations in $\frac{dy}{dx}$
  • Result: $\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{y - x^2}{y^2 - x}$
